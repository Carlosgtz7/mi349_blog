The Rise, Fall, and Rise Again of My Fantasy Football Team

September 23, 2025 | By Carlos Gutierrez

This year is my second time playing fantasy football. Last year I was in a 12 man league with my buddies and managed to have the best record in the league (12-2) despite hardly knowing anything about football. This year I was hoping to keep that momentum going. On draft night, positions were revealed and I had the first overall pick. Things were really looking good, especially being a 14 man league this year having the first pick becomes significantly more valuable. Overall I would say drafting went pretty well, I had some heavy hitters on my team such as Joe Burrow and Jamarr Chase with plenty of other solid players.

Fast forward to the week 1 of the season and things already took a turn. I took an unfortunate loss as many of my starters had horrible performances. Nothing to panic over of course, I lost week 1 last year and still finished 12-2 after all. It was week 2 where the team really crumbled before my eyes. Three of my starting players went down with serious injuries this week, one of them being Bengals quarterback Joe Burrow. This was the most devastating loss as without him the Bengals offense is significantly worse and I just so happen to have two more Bengals players on my roster.

At this point my team is in absolute shambles, so it was time to make some trades. In short, I made three trades where I essentially gave up Jamarr Chase, Kareem Hunt, Chris Godwin, and Bryce Young in exchange for Bijan Robinson, Tyreek Hill, Jameson Williams, Trevor Lawrence, and Bo Nix. To put into perspective how good these two trades were for my team I put together this chart below.

Trade Value Chart

Player Comparison Average Points Point Differential Notes
Bryce Young + Joe Burrow for Bo Nix 13.7 + 0 for 13.5 Very even trade, Bo Nix is just way more consistent than Young. Also Burrow is out for the season.
Jamarr Chase for Bijan Robinson 16.7 for 20.1 Not only does Bijan average more points, Jamarr's production will slow down since he has a backup quarterback throwing to him.
Hunt + Godwin for Hill + Williams 6.8 + 0 for 13.6 + 10.6 Godwin is set to come back from injury soon but had two ankle surgeries; Not likely going to be the same player.
Trevor Lawrence 12.4 Didn't give up anything for Lawrence, essentially got a backup quarterback for free.
